COMPUTER SCIENCE ENGINEERING

About the department :

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 1997. The Department offers qualitative training and scope for more research. The Dept. provides state-of- art computing facilities to the students. It also promotes active industry-institute collaboration by identifying areas of undergraduate and postgraduate degree students. The Courses offered in the department meet the requirements of both industry and research. The curriculum is designed to train the students in developing application programs (Software) and also designing and developing computing systems (Hardware).

CSE Mission & Vision :


Mission :
In order to prepare potential and productive persons in industry and academia as well as in government sector. in the graduate programs of the Department of CSE of JPNCE an excellent environment of teaching, learning, and research in computing.

In particular, we aim:
- To produce highly qualified and all-rounded graduate possessing in-depth knowledge of computing and information technology who can provide leadership and service to India and the world.
- To pursue creative research and new innovations in Computer Science and Engineering and across disciplines in order to serve the needs of industry, government, society, and the scientific community by expanding and contributing to the body of knowledge in the field.
- To develop strong partnerships with industrial and government and non-government agencies, professional societies, and local communities.

Vision:

To be recognized as the leader having proper and recent education and research in the Computer Science and Engineering arena, specially with a strong impact in the timely and targeted areas of research to attract the excellent faculty and students worldwide.

Educational Objectives

The academic objectives of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering are to provide outstanding education and research programs that can: - Promote a philosophy of learning with the development of a computer science engineering student body to be prepared for their specific profession, for advanced studies and careers in research with multidisciplinary knowledge.
- Provide a stimulating academic research environment for individuals who would be capable to work in both industry and academia, and would be the leaders in the demonstration of professional skills and in the development of new technology for the benefit of society.

DEPARTMENT

In the context of Indian contribution in the area of computer application in India as well as in global level, the department of Computer Science and Engineering has got a pivotal role to play in producing competent and committed computer professionals.

Our Department is supported by disciplined and dedicated faculty. They always come forward to enhance the Excellency of the department academically. We own a crowning glory in the list of its achievements. Backed with this academic excellence, more than 70% of our students are being placed in different reputed MNC’s every year, setting bench marks in the campus recruitments.

The department is headed by Dr. K. Guru Raghavendra Reddy. The department has eminent, experienced and dynamic teaching faculty, to guide the students. Started with an intake of 60 in years 97-98, today, it has grown up to 120. It also consists of PG Course M.Tech (CSE) with an intake of 18 Our department own 750 higher and configured machines with all the required licenced software. Our department has the State-of-art kind of laboratories with a 24 hour Internet facility accessible to both faculty and students.We have a department library for faculty as well as students.

The faculty and students are encouraged to attend the workshops and seminars within the campus as well as organized by several institutions and JNTU. Many of our students won first and second prizes in various paper presentations held at various places.

Many of our students are well equipped with various latest Microsoft Technologies and other programming skills. They developed several app’s and successfully uploaded into Windows Store. From windows store , many people from various countries downloaded and used them.

Many our students have secured more than 90 percentage in recent Gate exam and also they obtain high scores in GRE,TOEFL&IELTS exams.

The students made habituation of doing in house project as a part of their academics. The faculty and students are encouraged to attend the workshops and seminars within the campus as well as organized by several institutions and JNTU. Some of the practices of the department like teaching with lesson plans ,conduction of regular tests, mentoring activities, focusing on weak students, conduction of department level placement, supporting activities like conducting mock test, interviews and group discussion have helped the students to achieve greater heights in their academics and placement activities to add value to their education.
