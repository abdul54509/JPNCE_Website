Grievance Redressal Cell

  • The Grievance Redressal Cell attempts to address genuine problems and complaints of students whatever be the nature of the problem
  • The college assures students that once a complaint is made, it will be treated with sensitivity and confidentiality.
  • They may also approach the members of the cell or any of their other teachers as is comfortable to them.
