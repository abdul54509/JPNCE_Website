WHY STUDY AT JPNCE?

  • Dedicated Faculties

    Our Career Prep teachers and staff are dedicated to helping you succeed both academically and personally. No matter what barriers stand between you

  • Skill Development Activities

    We address the opportunities and challenges to encounter new demands of changing global and innovative technologies.


  • Excellent Placement Record

    All reputed organizations from the core industry, some of which are MNCs, come to the College for campus recruitment and assure that each eligible student gets an opportunity to be placed in a recognized firm.

  • Disciplined Environment

    A disciplined environment helps put both College and Students on their best behavior.Students who enter this type of administration know what the expectations are.

  • Industry Interaction & Consultancy Work

    We possess Civil Consultancy Cell, Energy Management Cell & ASWDC which provide a platform for students to enhance their technical & industrial skills.

  • Extra curricular

    Several clubs are active on the campus, initiated by enthusiastic students with a passion for the arts, culture, and sports. Student clubs like the sports, music, dance, literature, technology, NCC etc
