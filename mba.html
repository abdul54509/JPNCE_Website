DEPARTMENT

Engineers often become the prime managers of change within a business. Having already obtained a technical degree Or a General Degree young Graduates can speedily progress in their careers if they methodically study the aspects of management.

Realizing this fact, JAYA PRAKASH NARAYAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING established the department of MASTER OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION in 2006 with an intake of 60 students.

The department is headed by Dr. M.Madhu Mohan with vast Corporate and teaching experience.

Three electives are being offered to prepare students to meet the market demands, viz., Finance, Human Resource Management, and Marketing.

Various activities like seminars, group discussions, debates, case studies, Business quiz, Mock interviews and classroom participation form part of the motivated diplomacy envisage for these escalating managers.
