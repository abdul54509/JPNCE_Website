DEPARTMENT

The department of Mechanical Engineering was established in 1997 with an intake of 40.The intake has developed from 40 in 1997 to 60 in 1998 to 90 in 2001 to 120 in 2011.Under Mechanical Department, with B.Tech Production specialization, 60 students got admitted in the year 2001,2002,2003,2004 and successfully completed their graduation.Our department also runs Diploma course with an intake of 60 since 2009 under II Shift Polytechnic.One of our mechanical department students, Mr. K.Shekhar Reddy secured highest marks among all the JNTUH affiliated college students and received Gold Medal from the Chancellor of JNTUH and Governor of Andhra Pradesh in 2005.

Our department is also running PG course, M.Tech.Thermal Engineering since 2011.Our mechanical department is headed by senior JNTUH professors including Dr. C. B. Krishnamurthy (Rtd. Professor, Dept. of Mech., JNTUH, Kukatpally), Prof. D. S. Shastry (Rtd. Professor, Dept. of Mech., JNTUH, Kukatpally), Dr. T. Annapurneshwar Rao ((Rtd. Professor, Dept. of Mech., Osmania University), Dr. S. Murali (Phd. From Osmainia University ), Dr. G. Parthasarthi (Ph.D from IIT-Kharagpur).

The Mechanical Dept. has senior and experienced faculty to teach subjects from I Year to Final year, who are master (M.Tech.) in various specializations like Thermal, Production, Machine Design, etc.The department is equipped with excellent laboratories for Diploma, B.Tech. and M.Tech courses listed below:

  1. Engineering Workshop. (Fitting, Carpentry, Tin Smithy, Black Smithy, Foundary, House wiring, Welding and Plumbing)
  2. Production Technology Lab.
  3. Thermal Engineering Lab.
  4. Strength of material Lab.
  5. Metallurgy Lab.
  6. Metrology Lab.
  7. Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulic Machinery Lab.
  8. Machine Tools Lab.
  9. Instrumentation Lab.
  10. Heat Transfer Lab.
  11. CAD/CAM Lab.
  12. Engineering Drawing Lab.
  13. Thermodynamics Lab
  14. Kinematics and Dynamics Lab
  15. Fuels Lab.
  16. Pneumatics Lab.
  17. Refrigeration & Air-condition Lab
  18. M.Tech. Thermal Engineering Lab.
  19. Computational Methods Lab.

The department is equipped with CNC Lathe, CNC Milling, Turret Lathe, Water Plasma Welding and Cutting, Wind Flow Tunnel (which is sanctioned from AICTE’s MODRAB scheme) to facilitate research and development activities in the related areas.The department also conducts workshops, seminars regularly for student and faculty, to improve personality development, soft skills, training skills, interview skills, etc.The department has undergone MOU with SAP through T.A.S.K in Material Management module, which will aid students for better placements after completing their graduation.
